Armour Piercing Ammo 5 Punkte (Points mein ich ..)

Armour piercing ammo is either projectiles with specially hardened tips in the case of projectile weapons or a super - charged power pack in the case of energy weapons. When you fire a shot using armour piercing ammo the weapon's save modifier is increased to -6 for that one shot only. Against vehicles the weapon's armour penetration is increased by an amount which varies according to the Strength of t´he weapon as shown below. The decision to use armour piercing ammo must be made before the dice is rolled to hit.
Weapon's Strength/Bonus Penetration Dice
1-3/+1D3
4-5/+1D6
6-7/+1D12
8-10/+1D20
