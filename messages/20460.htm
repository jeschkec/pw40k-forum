Aber, damit es nicht so arg schlimm ist (*g*), hab ich dir einen alten Text aus einem Orkbuch der 1st Edition abgeschrieben:

Progressive music is largely the domain of the Mekaniaks. They have the technical skill and apparatus for recording, sampling, synthesizing and mixing. They record battle chants, the squirl of Squigs, shouting choirs, and even sounds of battle. Meks with musical inclinations blend these sounds into their own discordant compositions, then blare them out on loadspeakers for the entertainment and declaration of their fellow Orks. Such popular events known as Koncerts. The style of music currently in vogue with most Meks ist known as Rokk. The most progressive Meks in the field of Orkish musik are those of the Goff clan, who specialise in the eerie sounds of Goffik Rokk. Another popular style incorporates the sounds of machinery, gunfire, and live recordings of Gargant construction; this style is known as 'Evvy Mettal...

aus: Waaargh! the Orks, Kapitel: ART and MUZIK
von: Nigel Stillman & Bryan Ansell, 1990
