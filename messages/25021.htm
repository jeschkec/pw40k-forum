also hier is mein Wissen über die Fire Hawks. Sehr viel mehr als da steht wirst auch nicht aus mir rausbekommen:

The Fire Hawks Chapter was founded in the 21st founding (also known as the Cursed Founding) immediately after the Age of Apostasy. During those troubled times, the Chapter's home world of Zhoros was destroyed by Thermal Bombs. Subsequent to that tragedy, the Chapter's fleet became its new home.

"The Fire Hawks fought many wars and won many battles during their existance." In 904 M41, the Chapter was drawn into the Fourth Quadrant Rebellion when one of their ships was captured by the Mantis Warriors Chapter. They immediately retalliated, and continued to serve in the war until 907 M41, when they and the Red Scorpions "were recalled to their normal service duties". When the Tyrant of Badab was finally defeated in 912 M41, the Fire Hawks, as well as other loyalist Chapters, were given salvage rights to the enemy's space fleets as reward.

The Fire Hawks draw there gene-seed from the Ultramarines, and were thus a Codex Chapter. The Chapter was thought lost during warp travel en route to 'Crows World' on 963 M41. In reality, the Chapter somehow contracted a mysterious sickness and have decided to keep their existence secret. They have radically changed their paint scheme, and are known by the name 'Legion of the Damned'.
