Any Simpsons/Ultramarine army of less than 10,000 pts must be led by Marnius Homer. He carries the following wargear:Wargear: Gauntlets of Ultrimar, Artificer armour, Terminator Honours, purity seals (borrowed from Flanders) and Duff Beer. He can also choose any wargear from the Simpsons Wargear List up to 300 pts.Special Rules

Invulnerable: Homer can (and has) take more damage than any other human or Space Marine ever! As such he has an invulnerable save of 2+.

Independant Character: Just like it sez in the 40K rulebook.

Intoxication: (Drunk!) Homer rolls D6 at the beginning of each round; on a roll of 3+ Homer becomes drunk from Duff™ Beer. His Leadership then goes to 10, he will never fall back or become pinned and automatically passes any Leadership tests he is called upon to make. He is immune to all Psycology.

"D'Oh!" Before setting up the opposing player rolls 2D6 and consults the following table:

2 No-Show. Homer got lost, wrong planet/battle/enemy.

3 Set up as normal.

4 Shortcut! As a result of Homer's shortcut the army arrives three rounds late. (Let us never speak of the shortcut agin.) His opponent gets to take three full turns as normal before Homer even sets up!\

5 No Duff: Eldar Pirates/Barney/Orks have made off with the last drop of Duff Beer! As a result no Beer will be avaliable for Homer or as wargear for the army. The only exception to this is Chief Librarian Barnius-he will always have Duff avaliable.

6 Homer's strategic brilliance results in his opponent deploying the army anywhere on the board!

7 Homer's shortcut places his army in the same deployment zone with his opponent!

8 Set up as normal.

9 Shortcut through the Warp. Roll a D6 for each charicter and unit in the army. On a D6 roll of 6 the unit/charicter is lost in the warp and unavalible for this battle. Homer is unaffected by this rule and will always be present.

10 Teleport 'Homer'. Only homer deploys to the battle. The telporter malfunctions and Homer is placed in the center of the enemy deployment zone and must fight the battle alone.

11 Set up as normal.

12 Homer's strategic brilliance results in him deploying his opponents army anywhere on the board!
