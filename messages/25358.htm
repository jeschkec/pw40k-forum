Fearless- All Legion Units auto-pass all Ld tests they are required to take.

Terrifying-Any unit that loses combat to Legion take the test at an ADDITIONAL -1.

Where they come from, Nobody Knows- Come from nowhere, blah blah, reign firey death on all who oppose them, deep strike even when the mission does not allow them to, start rolling on the first turn for the Reserves rather than the second (I.E. turn 1=4+ turn 2=3+ turn 3=2+)

Legion Commander-70 points

WS=5 BS=5 S=4 T=4 W=3 I=5 A=4(!) LD=10 Sv3+

Options: any Codex: Space Marine stuff except Terminator Armor(ye gods!!!) and Terminator Honors.

Independant Character

Command Squad (as in able to have one)


Command Squad = 25 points (+15 Veteran Sergeant)
Stats are the same as Veteran Space Marines 4's all across except 2 Attacks and 9 LD(and 1 wound obviously) a veteran sergeant has 3 attacks.

Whole squad is 4 to 9 plus sergeant as per usual, the Squad can go in a rhino at +60 and can get an Apothecary, Techmarine or Standard Bearer at +15 Apiece.
2 Legionaires can have
H bolter +15
Lascannon +35
Missile Launcher +20
Flamer +3
Plasma +6
Meltagun +10
Squad can have frag and/or Krak for 1 and 2 points per, respectively.


Legionaire Squad-

Same all around except you can only have one heavy and one Special, you can still get the sergeant and rhino.


A Space Marine army may include a single Legion of the Damned squad as an Elites choice.

20 Points per Marine

Troop Type WS BS S T W I A Ld Sv
Marine 5 4 4 4 1 5 1 8 3+

Squad: The squad consists of between 5 and 10 Legion of the Damned Space Marines.

Weapons: Bolters. Any model may replace their Bolter with a Bolt Pistol and Close Combat Weapon at no extra cost.

Options: One model in the squad may be armed with one of the following weapons: Heavy Bolter +5, Missile Launcher +10, or Lascannon +15. In addition, one Marine in the squad may be armed with one of the following weapons: Meltagun +10, Flamer +6, or Plasma Gun +6. The entire squad may be given Frag Grenades at +1 point per model and Krak Grenades at +2 points per model.

Special Rules

Apparitions: The Legion of the Damned appear from nowhere, completely unheralded. In every battle they use the special Deep Strike deployment rules, even if the mission does not normally allow troops to Deep Strike. At the end of every Space Marine turn after the Legion of the Damned arrive, roll a D6. On a roll of a 1, the Legion of the Damned disappear as eerily as they came – remove the models from the table immediately – Victory Points are earnt according to their strength when they disappear (if only half of them are left, your opponent scores half the unit’s points value in Vistory Points, etc. . .) The Legion of the Damned may not be joined by an Independent Character, they always fight alone.

Fearless: It is debatable whether the Legion of the Damned are truly alive and have real minds. Legion of the Damned automatically pass any Leadership-based tests, including Morale Checks, that they are required to make.

Terrifying: Any unit which loses an assault whilst fighting the Legion of the Damned suffers an additional –1 modifier to its Leadership for the subsequent Morale Check.

Source-WD 228
