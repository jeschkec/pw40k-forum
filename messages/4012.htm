Callidus Assassin 120 points

C'Tan Phase Sword: No armour saves are allowed against the sword, even Invulnerable ones.

Neural Shredder: The Neural Shredeer uses the target's leadership to wound, rather than its Toughness. Vehicles suffer D3 Glancing Hits. Range: Template
Strength: 8 AP:1 Assault:1

Polymorphine: As main rulebook, page 263.

Poison Blades: If the Callidus is in contact with an enemy model at the end of an assault phase, she may attack with poison blades. She gets one attack, wounding on a 4+, normal saves allowed.

Jump Back: At the start of any Assault Phase, the Callidus may attempt to disengage from combat. Roll 1D6. On a 1 she fails. On 2+ she moves that many inches away from the enemy. This cannot be used to get into contact with another enemy. The enemy unit may consolidate after she does this.

A Word in Your Ear. . .: The owninjg player may move one enemy unit up to 6" after both sides have set up. This must be in its original deployment zone and the owning player may choose its facing.
