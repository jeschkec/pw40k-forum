Shadowsword - One Super Heavy Slot - 535 Punkte

FA: 14
SA: 13
RA: 12
BS: 3(4)

Type: Tank
Size: Super Heavy
Speed: Lumbering
Structure Points: 3
Crew: Imperial Guard (6)

Weapons:
The Shadowsword mounts a Volcano Cannon and 2x twin-linked heavy Bolters. Targeters (+1 BS shown above)

Options:
A Shadowsword may be given the following vehicles Upgrades for the points cost listed in the Imperial Guard armoury: hunter killer missle, improved comms, minesweeper, pintle-mounted storm bolter, searchlight, smoke launchers. In addition it can have a crew escape mechanism for 25 Points, which will save 2D3 crew Members instead of D3

Special Rules

Volcano Cannon:
Range: 120"
Strength: 10
AP: 2
Special: Ordnance1 / Blast, Titan Killer

Ordnance:
The Shadowsword may fire Ordnance and still fire other weapons.

Titan Killer:
Each hit inflicts D3 structure points damage on a target that has structure points. Roll seperately on the dammage tables for each point of damage.

Targeting:
Instead of picking one target for the SS (Shadowsword), pick a target for each of its weapons.

SS Tank Shock:
Enemy infantry must test at -1 to their Leadership if they are Tank Shocked by a SS

Lumbering Vehicle:
The SS can move up to 6" a turn. It must always move straight ahead, but can pivot by up to 90° at the end of the move.

Difficult Terrain:
The SS can ignore low walls, hedges, bushes and rubble. Going through other difficult terrain they test as normal. However, if they roll a 1 they ar e not automatically immobilisied, instead they lose D3" of movement, just as if they had suffered an Engines Damaged result on the damage tables.
