Having trouble applying transfers on the armour shoulder pads of space marines, or other non-flat surfaces, try the following:

Just apply the transfer as usual on the spot you want it to, and when it is in place take off any excess water that came with the transfer from the model with a washcloth or something else absorbing. then get your mum's hairdryer, and hold it close to the transferspot. Then turn it on for a second or 10, and after that press gently with your finger on the tranfer. Repeat this step for about 3 times, and you'll see that the transfer 'shrunk' into the shape of the shoulder pad e.d. Now the transfer will look like it was painted on the model!

WARNING: If the hairdryer is used too much then some parts of the model (especially small parts like the armour shoulder pads!) may shrink or even melt.
