Mit Sicherheit gibt es dazu auch die Entsprechung im deutschen WD:
>Space Marine Command Groups
>WD 250. An Apothecary or Techmarine from a Space Marines Command Squad may be assigned to any one Space Marine infantry or bike squad before the battle. The character counts as part of the unit and may not leave it during the game. If one of these characters is assigned to an Assault Squad, he must be equipped with a jump pack (+10 points). If he is assigned to a Bike or a Scout Bike Squadron, he must be mounted on a bike (+20 points). Alternatively, he may be mounted on modified Attack Bike, replacing the gunner in the sidecar (+30 points). All the usual benefits are conferred to the character, such as increased Movement, better Toughness, and a better saving throw. If a Scout Squad is joined by an Apothecary or Techmarine, the squad may not Infiltrate (no sneaking past the enemy sentries, for a Space Marine is clad in heavy and cumbersome power armour).
